Radiation (energy movement, charge acceleration, energytransport, poynting)

Hypothesis: Accelerated charge causes radiation (analogous to “force”)?

  1. Optical Radiation spectral range: \(\lambda = \left[ 100nm, 1mm \right]\):
    1. high frequency: UV spectral range: \(\lambda = \left[ 100nm, 200nm \right]\)
    2. visibility wavelength: light (Photon) range: \(\lambda = \left[ 380nm, 780nm \right]\)
    3. long wavelength: IR spectral range: \(\lambda = \left[ 5\cdot10^4nm, 10^6nm \right]\)
  2. Heat Radiation invisible (Black Body “B”), \(\mathscr{L}_{\lambda,B}(\lambda,T)\)

Charge: \[\begin{split} & q = ne_0 \qquad \sum{q} = Q = I \cdot t \qquad n \in \mathbb{Z} \end{split} \tag{1}\]

Radiation: \[\begin{split} & \mathscr{S} = \frac{d}{dt}p = \frac{1}{c} \frac{d}{dt}(E_{kin}) \stackrel{?}{\propto} q \cdot a \end{split} \tag{2}\]

Radiation orientation angles for optics and thermodynamics:

Optical Radiation:

Deviation angle of ray beam after refraction (transmission) and diffraction:

\[\begin{split} & \varsigma = \alpha + arcsin \left[ sin(\varpi) \sqrt{\eta^2-sin^2(\alpha)} - cos(\varpi)sin(\alpha) \right] - \varpi = \alpha + \alpha_2 - \varpi \end{split} \tag{3}\]

where \(\varpi = \beta + \beta_2 = const.\)

Minimal aberration (minimal projection error):

\(\varsigma = \varsigma_{min} \Longleftrightarrow\)

\[\begin{split} & \varsigma_{min} \Longleftrightarrow \\ \frac{d}{d\alpha} \varsigma = 0 \ \wedge \\ \varsigma_{min} = 2\alpha - \varpi = 2(\alpha - \beta) \end{split} \tag{4}\]

Thermal Radiation:

Spectral Reflection: \[\begin{split} & \mathscr{R}(\lambda) = \frac{d^3\Phi_{\lambda,r}}{d^3\Phi_{\lambda,e}} \end{split} \tag{5}\]

Spectral Transmission: \[\begin{split} & \mathscr{T}(\lambda) = \frac{d^3\Phi_{\lambda,t}}{d^3\Phi_{\lambda,e}} \end{split} \tag{6}\]

Spectral Absorbtion: \[\begin{split} & \mathscr{A}(\lambda) = \frac{d^3\Phi_{\lambda,a}}{d^3\Phi_{\lambda,e}} \end{split} \tag{7}\]

Spectral Emission: \[\begin{split} & \mathscr{E}(\lambda) = \frac{\mathscr{L}(\lambda)}{\mathscr{L}(\lambda,B)} \end{split} \tag{8}\]

\(\mathscr{R}(\lambda) + \mathscr{T}(\lambda) + \mathscr{A}(\lambda) = 1\)

Black Body “B”: \[\begin{split} & \mathscr{A}(\lambda,T) = \mathscr{E}(\lambda,T) = 1,\ \mathscr{R}(\lambda,T) = 0,\ \mathscr{T}(\lambda,T) = 0 \end{split} \tag{9}\]

\(\Rightarrow\) Heat Radiation (Black Body):

\[\begin{split} & \mathscr{L}_{B} = \frac{c}{4\pi} \langle w_{EM} \rangle = \frac{c}{4\pi} w_B \end{split} \tag{10}\]

\[\begin{split} & \mathscr{L}_{\lambda,B}(\lambda,T) = \frac{\mathscr{L}_{\lambda}(\lambda,T)}{\mathscr{A}_{\lambda}(\lambda,T)} \stackrel{continuum}{=} \frac{2c}{\lambda^4}kT \stackrel{quantum}{=} \frac{2hc^2}{\lambda^5} \frac{1}{e^{\frac{hc}{\lambda kT}}-1} = const. \end{split} \tag{11}\]

\[\begin{split} & \mathscr{L}_{\nu,B}(\nu,T) \stackrel{quantum}{=} \frac{2h\nu^3}{c^2} \frac{1}{e^{\frac{hc}{kT}}-1} \end{split} \tag{12}\]

\[\begin{split} & \mathscr{L}_{\lambda,B}(\lambda_x,T_x) = \left( \frac{T_x}{T} \right)^5 \cdot \mathscr{L}_{\lambda,B}(\lambda,T) \end{split} \tag{13}\]

\(T_x > T \Rightarrow \lambda_x < \lambda\)

\[\begin{split} & \lambda_{max} \cdot T = \frac{h \cdot c}{5k} \frac{1}{1 - e^{-\lambda_{max} \cdot T}} = b = 2.897773 \cdot 10^{-3} m \cdot K \end{split} \tag{14}\]

Heat Radiation Transfer (sent from source \(g\) to receiver \(b\) with areas \(\mathscr{A}\)):

\[\begin{split} & d^2\Phi \stackrel{vacuum}{=} \mathscr{L} \frac{dA_g \cos(\varepsilon_g)}{R} \frac{dA_b \cos(\varepsilon_b)}{R} = \frac{\mathscr{L}}{R^2} \cdot dA_g \cos(\varepsilon_g) \cdot dA_b \cos(\varepsilon_b) = \mathscr{L} \cos(\varepsilon_b) dA_b d\Omega_b \end{split} \tag{15}\]

Heat Radiation Return, Reflection Absorbtion (Feedback absorbed “\(a\)” at sender \(g\) received “\(e\)” at receiver \(b\), with areas \(\mathscr{A}\) at wavelength \(\lambda\) ):

\[\begin{split} & d^3\Phi_{\lambda,a,b} \stackrel{vacuum}{=} \mathscr{A}_b \cdot d^3\Phi_{\lambda,e,b} = \mathscr{A}_b \cdot \mathscr{L}_{\lambda} \cos(\varepsilon_b) d\mathscr{A}_b d\Omega_b d\lambda = \mathscr{L}_{\lambda,b} \cos(\varepsilon_g) d\mathscr{A}_g d\Omega_g d\lambda = d^3\Phi_{\lambda,b} \end{split} \tag{16}\]

Energy Density w and Pressure \(P\), Temperature \(T\), Volume \(V\), inner Energy \(U\), Entropy density \(s\), Radiance

\(\mathscr{L}_B\):

\[\begin{split} & w_B = \frac{\mathscr{L}_B}{c} \int\limits_0^{4\pi} d\Omega = \frac{4\pi}{c} \cdot \mathscr{L}_B = \frac{4}{c} \cdot \mathscr{M}_B = 2 \cdot 2\pi \cdot \frac{\mathscr{L}_B}{c} \stackrel{oriented}{=} P_{EM} \stackrel{unoriented}{=} 3 w_{EM} = 3 \left( \varepsilon_0 \varepsilon_r \mathscr{E}^2 \right) = 3 P_B = 3 \left( - \frac{dW}{dV} \right)_B = 3 \left( - \frac{d(F \cdot x)}{dV} \right)_B = 3 \left( - \frac{m}{V} \cdot x \cdot \ddot{x} \right)_B = T \frac{\partial P}{\partial T} - P = \frac{\partial U}{\partial V} = aT^4 = \frac{3}{4} T \cdot s_B \end{split} \tag{17}\]

\[\begin{split} & w_{\lambda,B} \stackrel{continuum}{=} - \frac{dn}{d\lambda} \overline{W} = \frac{8\pi}{\lambda^4}kT \stackrel{quantum}{=} \frac{8\pi hc}{\lambda^5} \frac{1}{e^{\frac{hc}{kT}}-1} = 8\pi h \nu \frac{1}{\lambda^4} \frac{1}{e^{\frac{hc}{kT}}-1} \end{split} \tag{18}\]

\[\begin{split} & w_{\nu,B} \stackrel{continuum}{=} \frac{dn}{d\nu} \overline{W} = \frac{8\pi\nu^2}{c^3}kT \stackrel{quantum}{=} \frac{8\pi h\nu^3}{c^3} \frac{1}{e^{\frac{hc}{kT}}-1} = 8\pi h \left(\frac{1}{\lambda}\right)^3 \frac{1}{e^{\frac{hc}{kT}}-1} \end{split} \tag{19}\]

where Radiant Exitance (Emittance) \(\mathscr{M}_B = \frac{d\Phi}{dA_g} = \sigma T^4\) and proportionality constants:

\(a = \frac{2^3}{3\cdot5} \frac{\pi^5 k^4}{h^3 c^3} = 7.5657 \cdot 10^{-16}Jm^{-3}K^{-4}\) (\(a\) = constant from integration)

\(\sigma = \frac{1}{4} \cdot a \cdot c = \frac{2}{3\cdot5} \frac{\pi^5 k^4}{h^3 c^3} = 5.6704 \cdot 10^{-8}Wm^{-2}K^{-4}\) (\(c\) = light speed)

\[\begin{split} & \frac{d}{d\Omega} P_{EM} \stackrel{oriented}{=} \frac{d}{d\Omega} w_B = \frac{\mathscr{L}_B}{c} \end{split} \tag{20}\]

Blackbody Entropy Density \(s_B = \frac{4}{3} aT^4\)

Blackbody Entropy \(S_B = \frac{4}{3} aVT^3\)

Blackbody Inner Energy \(U_B = aVT^4\)

Blackbody Pressure \(P_B = \frac{1}{3} aT^4 \stackrel{unoriented}{=} \frac{1}{3} w_B\)
